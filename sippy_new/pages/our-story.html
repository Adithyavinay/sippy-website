Our Story

Built from a real problem.
Made for real life.

Sippy didn't start in a boardroom. It started with a bad cup of hotel coffee, a train journey through Hyderabad, and one simple question — why can't hot water just come with me?

"I was tired of depending on a socket, a canteen, or someone else just to get a hot drink. I wanted something that was mine, portable, and it just worked."

— Founder, Sippy India · Hyderabad

01
The Problem

A kettle that only works
when plugged in?

Anyone who has stayed in a hostel, traveled by train overnight, or spent long hours at a desk knows the struggle. You want hot water — for tea, for instant noodles, for a warm drink at 2 AM — and there's just no easy way to get it.

Hotel rooms don't always have kettles. Canteens close early. The pantry car is four bogies away. And those plastic travel mugs? They barely hold heat, let alone boil water.

That was our everyday reality in Hyderabad. And we knew we weren't alone.

🔌
The Old Way Find a socket. Find a kettle.
Hope the voltage works.
02
The Search

We looked everywhere.
Nothing fit.

We searched for a portable kettle that was actually portable — not just a smaller version of the same corded appliance. We wanted something rechargeable, genuinely wireless, food-safe, and compact enough to go in a backpack.

The international options were expensive and not designed for Indian travel needs. The cheap ones online were plastic, poorly made, and nowhere near safe.

So we thought — why not build one ourselves? One that Indians would actually trust and use every single day.

🔍
12+ Months of research, sampling,
and testing products
03
The Solution

Meet Sippy.
Hot water. Anywhere.

After months of sampling, testing, and going back and forth with manufacturers, we landed on the Sippy Smart Kettle — a 300ml, USB-C rechargeable, wireless kettle made from 316 food-grade stainless steel.

No cord. No socket. No dependency. Just charge it, fill it, and have hot water wherever you are — on a train, in a hostel room, at your desk, or on a trek.

Rs. 5,999. Free delivery. Made for India.
